Easy Madeleine Recipe

It is so simple: You can make the batter within 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Granulated sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla beans
  • Lemon zest
  • Whole eggs
  • Honey
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Cake flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, salt, vanilla beans, and lemon zest in a bowl.
  2. Add eggs, honey, milk, and oil
  3. Add sifted cake flour and baking powder.
  4. Add warm melted butter
  5. Let it rest in the fridge
  6. Pipe the batter
  7. Bake at 365 F | 185 C
